About Us

MARF was founded with a vision to bridge the gap between farmers and modern agricultural machinery. We aim to empower small and medium-scale farmers by making high-quality equipment accessible, affordable, and easy to rent. Through our cloud-based platform, farmers can connect with equipment owners and hire machinery as per their needs. Our system also allows owners to earn extra income by listing idle equipment for rent. MARF offers multilingual support, secure payments, real-time booking, and verified operator services. We ensure that even farmers in remote areas can leverage the benefits of modern mechanization. Our platform promotes transparency, flexibility, and convenience in agricultural rentals. With smart features like dynamic pricing, usage insights, and seasonal discounts, MARF maximizes productivity. We also foster a growing community of farmers and experts to share knowledge and best practices. At MARF, we believe technology should serve the hands that feed us — the farmers.

Knowledge Hub

5 Tips for Efficient Tilling

Tilling prepares the soil for sowing, but doing it right boosts results. Use appropriate depth, maintain consistent speed, and avoid extreme soil conditions. Keep your tiller well-maintained to ensure efficiency.

How to Choose the Right Sprayer

Choosing the right sprayer depends on your crop and field size. Check for tank capacity, spray range, and nozzle types. Battery sprayers work well for small farms. Prioritize durability and safety.

Best Practices for Harvesters

Start with a well-calibrated harvester to reduce crop loss. Clean regularly, use the right header, and operate at optimal speed. Routine maintenance is key to longevity and reliability.
